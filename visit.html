SPORT CLUB MANAGEMENT SYSTEM

Introduction

In sports we learn to relate ourselves with other people who have similar interest and a common goal to achieve. It does not matter the sport, whether it is an individual or a team sport, there are always people around us that get directly or indirectly to make it possible. Despite the most visual part of the sports, there are also things that are not as easily seen and require a huge amount of work. The bigger the club is the more increases the effort required for this task, and having the data organized and accessible is fundamental.

Purpose of the Sport Club Program

The Sport Club program at Xavier International College offers the community an opportunity to participate in a broad variety of sports and recreational activities. All programs are based on members' interest and designed to enhance the experience by creating an environment where students can unite in diverse groups to achieve common goals and objectives while encouraging healthy lifestyles. Sport Clubs provide a valuable learning experience through student involvement in fundraising, public relations, organization, administration, budgeting, scheduling, teaching, and leadership development. The clubs with the most success and stability are those with active members and dedicated officers

Role of Board Members and Directors

The admin and board members serves primarily as an advisor and resource person for SportClub leaders and is available to assist student representatives with club business. It is the board members'/ admin's responsibility to monitor club activities to ensure all procedures are being followed. They shall recommend decisions to the Sport Club Council on items not specifically covered in this manual.

Responsibilities of Club

    Makes almost all preceedings automated.
    Conducts matches with teams of other clubs at least once a month.
    Assist the players to make connections with sponsors.
    Recommend revisions of the Sport Club Manual and procedures for the following year.
    Review and approve people seeking new admittance and/or reinstatement into the program.
    Arranges events where members as well as non-members can attend.
    All recommendations and/or actions are subject to approval or rejection by the Admins or Board Members.
